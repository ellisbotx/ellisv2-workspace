## üîÑ Mastermind Session Protocol

### When to Convene

Invoke the Mastermind when Marco faces:
- Any decision involving $10K+ in capital
- New product lines or brand extensions
- Channel expansion (retail, DTC, international)
- Major operational changes (new suppliers, fulfillment shifts)
- Strategic pivots or annual planning
- When something "feels right" but the numbers aren't clear yet

### ‚ö° Quick Mode (3 Personas, ~5 Minutes)

**Use for:** Weekly decisions, tactical moves, gut-check moments

**Process:**
1. **Frame it** ‚Äî Marco states the decision in one sentence
2. **Pick 3** ‚Äî Select the 3 most relevant personas (or Ellis picks based on the decision type)
3. **Lightning round** ‚Äî Each persona gives their take in 2-3 sentences
4. **Tension check** ‚Äî Where do they disagree?
5. **Call it** ‚Äî One clear recommendation with the key risk to watch

**Example trigger phrases:**
- "Run this through the mastermind quick"
- "What would the council say about..."
- "Quick gut check from the advisors"

### üî¨ Deep Mode (All 7 Personas, Full Analysis)

**Use for:** Quarterly strategy, major investments, pivotal decisions

**Process:**

**Phase 1 ‚Äî Present the Decision (Marco)**
- What's the decision?
- What are the options?
- What data do we have?
- What's the timeline?
- What's at stake (upside and downside)?

**Phase 2 ‚Äî Individual Counsel (Each Persona)**
- Each advisor weighs in from their unique lens
- Specific recommendations, not vague principles
- Each identifies the ONE thing Marco is probably not seeing

**Phase 3 ‚Äî Identify Tensions**
- Where do advisors directly contradict each other?
- What assumptions differ between their recommendations?
- Which tensions are real trade-offs vs false dichotomies?

**Phase 4 ‚Äî Synthesis**
- Clear recommendation with confidence level (high/medium/low)
- Key risks identified with mitigation strategies
- Specific next action with timeline
- What would change the recommendation (kill criteria)

**Phase 5 ‚Äî Decision Record**
- Document: decision, reasoning, what we expect to happen, when to revisit
- Save to `/data/decisions/` for future reference
